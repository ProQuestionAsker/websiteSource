The Premise

As a dog-lover living in Seattle, I have become familiar with how difficult it can be to adopt a dog in the city. Dogs are so popular that shelters and rescues in Seattle often import adoptable dogs from other states and sometimes other countries. After realizing that this information wasnâ€™t commonly known or understood, I decided to find a way to quantify how many dogs are moved before adoption and where they were moved to and from.

Ultimately, I used data from PetFinder to track the 2,460 dogs that were available for adoption in a different location from where they originated.
